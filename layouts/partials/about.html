Unfortunately, we have decided to cancel VRIPHYS’19.

We do not believe that we can offer a program that meets the level of quality expected by the physics simulation and computer graphics community due to an unusually low number of paper submissions.

We would like to thank the authors that did submit papers for their time and effort in preparing their submissions. We also thank the international program committee and the workshop organizers of Eurographics 2019 for their help during preliminary stages of planning the workshop.

Sincerely,
Sheldon, Kenny, Fabrice, and Gabriel
VRIPHYS’19 Workshop Chairs
